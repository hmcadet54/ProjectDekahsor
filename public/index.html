Our Story

"Reigniting Amir-e-Kabir's Purpose in the Modern Era, believing in his openness and collaboration"

DE KOSHUR CRAFTS didn't just outreached Kashmiri handicrafts. We actively sought opportunities to collaborate with businesses in the USA. DKC's mission-driven approach has paid off in ways, we could have never imagined. Let's break the mold of cutthroat competition and invite others to join us on this journey. We raised $1.3 million as business capital for the upliftment of Kashmir art and craft. This significant milestone meant more than just funds; it symbolized hope, dedication, and the power of community. The small community of Kashmiri immigrants in the U.S. had shown that even from thousands of miles away, they could make a profound impact on the lives of their brethren in Kashmir. Amir-e-Kabir's dream needs to illuminate the path for generations to come.
